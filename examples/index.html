deck-wind-layer: an animated WebGL wind map layer for deck.gl v9

A small MIT npm package that renders a Windy-style wind field: particles advected on the GPU through a u/v wind texture, drawn as fading comet trails, projected through deck's project32 so they stay glued to the map at any zoom or pan. On-screen particle density holds constant as you zoom.
